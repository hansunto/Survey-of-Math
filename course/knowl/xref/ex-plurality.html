Example 42.

Table 40 shows the preference table for the four candidates running for student body president. Who is declared the winner using the Plurality method?
🔗
Solution.
A received 130 first-place votes, D received \(120+100=220\) first-place votes and C received 150 first-place votes. Thus Daniel is the winner.
