    1.

    Suppose a local sales tax rate is \(4.5\%\) and you purchase a car for \(\$28,500\text{.}\)
    1. How much sales tax is paid?
      🔗
      🔗
    2. How much is the total cost of the car?
      🔗
      🔗
    🔗
    Answer.
    1. \(\displaystyle \$1,282.50\)
      🔗
      🔗
    2. \(\displaystyle \$29,782.50\)
      🔗
      🔗
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    1. Tax = \(28500 \times 0.045 = 1282.50\text{.}\)
      🔗
      🔗
    2. Total = \(28500 + 1282.50 = 29782.50\text{.}\)
      🔗
      🔗
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    2.

    A bank offers a CD that pays a simple interest rate of \(5.25\%\text{.}\) How much must you put in this CD now in order to have \(\$5,000\) for college tuition in five years?
    🔗
    Answer.
    \(\$3,960.40\)
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    Use \(A = P(1 + rt)\text{.}\) \(5000 = P(1 + 0.0525 \times 5)\) \(5000 = P(1.2625)\) \(P = 5000 / 1.2625 \approx 3960.40\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    3.

    How much money should be deposited today in an account that earns \(5\%\) compounded quarterly so that it will accumulate to \(\$15,000\) in ten years?
    🔗
    Answer.
    \(\$9,126.10\)
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    Use \(P = A(1 + r/n)^{-nt}\text{.}\) \(P = 15000(1 + 0.05/4)^{-40}\) \(P = 15000(1.0125)^{-40} \approx 9126.10\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    4.

    The cost of washer-dryer is \(\$1,100\text{.}\) We can finance this by paying \(\$100\) down and \(\$110\) per month for 12 months. Determine the finance charge.
    🔗
    Answer.
    \(\$320\)
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    Total paid = Down payment + (Monthly payment \(\times\) months) Total = \(100 + (110 \times 12) = 100 + 1320 = 1420\text{.}\) Finance Charge = \(1420 - 1100 = 320\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    5.

    At the time of her grandson’s birth, a grandmother deposits \(\$5,000\) in an account that pays \(6\%\) compounded monthly. What will be the value of the account at the child’s twenty-first birthday?
    🔗
    Answer.
    \(\$17,560.10\)
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    \(A = 5000(1 + 0.06/12)^{(12 \times 21)}\) \(A = 5000(1.005)^{252} \approx 17560.10\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    6.

    You deposit \(\$7,500\) in an account that pays \(4.75\%\) interest compounded monthly. Find the future value after one year and the effective annual yield.
    🔗
    Answer.
    FV: \(\$7,864.08\text{;}\) Yield: \(4.85\%\)
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    FV = \(7500(1 + 0.0475/12)^{12} \approx 7864.08\text{.}\) Yield = \((1 + 0.0475/12)^{12} - 1 \approx 0.0485\) or \(4.85\%\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    7.

    Find the value of an annuity of \(\$1,000\) at the end of every 6 months at \(4.5\%\) compounded semiannually for 10 years.
    🔗
    Answer.
    Value: \(\$24,911.52\text{;}\) Interest: \(\$4,911.52\)
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    Use \(A = P \frac{(1+r/n)^{nt}-1}{r/n}\text{.}\) \(A = 1000 \frac{(1+0.045/2)^{20}-1}{0.045/2} \approx 24911.52\text{.}\) Total deposits = \(1000 \times 20 = 20000\text{.}\) Interest = \(24911.52 - 20000 = 4911.52\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    8.

    Financial goal of \(\$150,000\) at \(4.75\%\) compounded quarterly for 35 years. Find the periodic deposit.
    🔗
    Answer.
    Deposit: \(\$168.32\)
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    \(P = \frac{A(r/n)}{(1+r/n)^{nt}-1}\) \(P = \frac{150000(0.0475/4)}{(1+0.0475/4)^{140}-1} \approx 168.32\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    9.

    Loan of \(\$36,000\) at \(8\%\) for 10 years.
    1. Find monthly payments and total interest.
      🔗
      🔗
    2. Compare to a 5-year term.
      🔗
      🔗
    🔗
    Answer.
    1. Payment: \(\$436.78\text{;}\) Interest: \(\$16,413.60\)
      🔗
      🔗
    2. Pay \(\$293.10\) more per month; Save \(\$8,620.80\) interest.
      🔗
      🔗
    🔗
    🔗
    Solution.
    Use \(PMT = \frac{Pr/n}{1-(1+r/n)^{-nt}}\text{.}\)
    🔗
    10-yr: \(PMT \approx 436.78\text{.}\) Total paid = \(52413.60\text{.}\) Interest = \(16413.60\text{.}\)
    🔗
    5-yr: \(PMT \approx 729.88\text{.}\) Total paid = \(43792.80\text{.}\) Interest = \(7792.80\text{.}\)
